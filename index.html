ceLoot represents a basic set of traits of character on the Celo network. It is the beginning of the D'n'D metaverse. New traits can be earned, won, or bought. ceLoot will contain a more advanced set of traits after interactions with other contracts.

Contrary to projects like Loot, ceLoot allows expanding imagination in MORE game cases. People like to play or associate themself not only with the fairytale world but also with the world of money-making or development.

There will be many projects using the underlying contract. Users can create their own stories, interpret characters according to their traits. We look forward to cooperating with more projects and building Metaverse together.
